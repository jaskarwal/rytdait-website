Privacy

Delete your account

Request deletion of your RytDait account and associated data.

Delete in the app (fastest)

  1. Open the RytDait app and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → My Account → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm deletion. Your account and associated data are permanently deleted.

Request by email

If you cannot use the app, email us to request deletion:

  1. Send an email to support@rytdait.com with subject Delete Account.
  2. Include the email address used for your RytDait account.
  3. We will verify ownership and delete your account and associated data within 30 days (usually sooner).

What is deleted

  • Account credentials and profile information
  • Health / preference data used for meal planning
  • Meal plans and meal logs stored for your account
  • Subscription status records we store for your account

App Store / Google Play billing history is managed by Apple or Google and may be retained by them under their policies. Cancel any active subscription in your store account settings to stop future charges.
